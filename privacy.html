Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

The short version Kassior does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial data on our end. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Who we are (data controller)

Kassior is an independent personal finance app based in Luzern, Switzerland. For all privacy-related matters, the responsible party is the operator reachable at hello@kassior.com. This policy applies to the Kassior web app and any native app versions distributed through app stores.

What data we collect

We collect nothing. Kassior does not send any data to our servers. There is no account system, no sign-in, and no usage tracking of any kind. No personal data is processed by us as a data controller.

Where your data lives

All data you enter in Kassior — expenses, income, bills, trips, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (browser localStorage). It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it yourself.

If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, your Kassior data is permanently deleted from your device. We have no copy of it and cannot recover it.

Exporting and importing

Kassior lets you export your data as a JSON or CSV file. This export is created on your device and saved wherever you choose — we do not receive or have access to it. If you import a backup, the data is read locally and never transmitted.

Google Fonts

Kassior loads typefaces (DM Sans and Outfit) from Google Fonts. When your device fetches these font files, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers as part of a standard web request. This is the only external network request Kassior makes, and it is unrelated to your financial data.

We load these fonts on the basis of our legitimate interest in providing a consistent visual experience. We have no control over Google's processing of your IP address. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy. If you prefer no external requests at all, you can use Kassior in a browser with network access blocked for fonts.googleapis.com — the app remains fully functional.

Cookies and tracking

Kassior does not use cookies, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any form of behavioural analytics. The only browser storage we use is localStorage for your app data.

Third parties

Kassior does not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party data processors beyond the font loading described above. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

Children

Kassior is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including minors. If you are under 16, please use the app only with parental awareness.

Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP), you have rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability of your personal data. Because Kassior holds no personal data about you on our end, these rights apply directly to the data on your own device — which is entirely under your control. To delete your data, clear your browser's local storage or uninstall the app.

For any privacy enquiry or rights request, contact hello@kassior.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back periodically if you have questions about how your data is handled.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, email hello@kassior.com.
