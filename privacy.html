Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version Kassior does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Who we are

Kassior is a personal finance tracking app built and maintained as an independent project. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@kassior.com.

What data we collect

We collect nothing. Kassior does not send any data to external servers. There is no account system, no sign-in, and no usage tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

All data you enter in Kassior — expenses, income, bills, trips, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (browser localStorage or the equivalent on your platform). It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it yourself.

If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, your Kassior data is permanently deleted from your device. We have no copy of it.

Exporting and importing

Kassior lets you export your data as a JSON or CSV file. This export is created on your device and saved wherever you choose — we do not receive or have access to it. If you import a backup, the data is read locally and never transmitted.

Fonts

Kassior loads fonts (DM Sans and Outfit) from Google Fonts. When your device fetches these fonts, Google receives your IP address as part of a standard web request. This is the only external network request Kassior makes, and it is unrelated to your financial data. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third parties

Kassior does not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party data processors. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

Children

Kassior is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Your rights

Because we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. Your data is entirely under your control on your own device. If you want to delete it, clear your browser's local storage or uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Kassior after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, email us at hello@kassior.com.
