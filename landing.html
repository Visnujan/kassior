What's inside

Everything you need to understand your money.

No bank connection. No cloud. Just a clean, fast app that does what you tell it.

Overview
Net this month, spending pace, savings goals, and bill reminders — all on one screen. See your financial picture at a glance without digging through menus.
Full History
Every transaction, searchable and filterable. Swipe to delete, tap to edit. Deleted items go to trash for 30 days before they're gone for good.
Trips
Create a trip, log expenses on the go with per-expense currency selection, and get a full breakdown when you're back. Completely sandboxed — trip spending never touches your regular budget.
Bills
Monthly, yearly, or one-time. See what's due before it hits your account. Overdue bills show in red, upcoming in amber — no surprises.
Insights
Charts that show where your money actually goes. Monthly trends, category breakdowns, and comparisons — so you can spot patterns and spend smarter.
Action Button
Assign Kassior to your iPhone's Action Button or Back Tap. Log an expense before you even put your wallet away — the fastest expense tracker on iOS.
Privacy by design
Your data is
nobody else's
business.

Every other finance app needs an account because your data lives on their server. That means they can see it, lose it in a breach, sell it, or shut down and take it with them.

Kassior stores everything in your phone's local storage. I have no server. I literally cannot see your data. When you delete the app, everything is gone — which is a feature, not a bug.

Soon, Kassior will sync via iCloud (iOS) and Google Drive (Android) — your file moving between your own devices, still no server in the middle.
